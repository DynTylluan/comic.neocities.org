Q. "Public Domain"? What's that?

A. In a nutshell, Public Domain works have no Copyright. Works loose their Copyright, if

  • They were published in the U.S. before 1923
  • They were published with a copyright notice from 1923 through 1963 without copyright renewal
  • They were published without a copyright notice from 1923 through 1977
  • They were published without a copyright notice from 1978 through 1st of March 1989, and without subsequent registration within 5 years
